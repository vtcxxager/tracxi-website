Privacy Policy

TRACXI values your privacy. We collect location data and contact details purely to facilitate logistics matching between load providers and vehicle owners. Your data is encrypted and is never shared with third-party advertisers. By using the TRACXI application, you consent to our data handling practices designed for logistics efficiency.

Security Policy

Our platform implements end-to-end encryption for all communication. We verify listings to ensure a secure environment for transport professionals. Security audits are performed regularly to protect the TRACXI infrastructure from unauthorized access or data breaches.
